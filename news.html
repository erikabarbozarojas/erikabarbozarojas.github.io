Visit from our colleagues at Agroambiente Ecuador

Costa Rica Oct 25 2024

It was a pleasure to welcome our friends from AGROAMBIENTE-Ecuador, with whom we conducted technical visits to various projects in citrus, pineapple, and coffee. We thank engineers Guido Zurita and Andrés Zurita for the exchange of experiences and knowledge, and we look forward to continuing to strengthen our capacities in agricultural research and innovation.

AGROTRANSFORMACIÓN 2024

Costa Rica Jul 11 2024

The advisory team of Agromultiservicios participated in the 2024 edition of AGROTRANSFORMACIÓN, an event organized by the Foreign Trade Promoter (PROCOMER). At booth number 21, visitors had the opportunity to learn about our services and expand their network of contacts in research and regulatory affairs for agriculture in Costa Rica. The University EARTH hosted this important event.

 

AGRITRADE 2024

Guatemala 13 -15 Mar 2024

In the city of Antigua Guatemala, the most significant agri-food sector fair in Central America, AGRITRADE 2024, took place. This event provided us with an opportunity to offer our research and regulatory affairs services to new companies and strengthen commercial ties with existing clients. Additionally, the event facilitated working meetings to reaffirm the implementation of new projects aimed at importing Guatemalan-made bioinputs into our country.

 

VIII Edition of Enlace Comercial

Costa Rica Feb 20 2024

We were present at the VIII Edition of Enlace Comercial held at the Crown Plaza Hotel, Corobicí. The eighth edition of Enlace Comercial was organized by the Costa Rican Foreign Trade Chamber (Crecex), where we held meetings with the commercial offices of the embassies of Israel, Spain, India, the Netherlands, and the United Kingdom. This activity allowed us to introduce ourselves in other markets and strengthen relationships with countries such as Israel, with whom we already have an excellent commercial relationship.

 

35th anniversary of CIPROC

Costa Rica Set 2023

As part of the 35th anniversary of CIPROC at the University of Costa Rica, our team participated with a stand where event attendees had the opportunity to learn about our services and clarify any questions regarding the research and registration process of agricultural inputs in our country. It was a highly productive event, as we were able to expand our network of contacts and client portfolio.

 

Agromercados, Honduras

Honduras May 18-19 2023

Agromultiservicios participated in Agromercados, Honduras, an international fair organized by the Secretariat of Agriculture of Honduras, FUNDER (Foundation for Rural Business Development), and FENAGH (National Federation of Farmers and Ranchers of Honduras). This event serves as a pivotal gathering for diverse stakeholders in the agricultural sector—including producers, national and international buyers, agro-exporters, agro-industry, the financial sector, input suppliers, packaging and service providers, public entities, and cooperative ventures—to foster business opportunities. During the fair, we engaged with formulators and distributors of agricultural inputs, aiming to expand our market presence and stimulate research and registration of agricultural inputs that hold potential for the Costa Rican agriculture sector.

 

Costa Rica 16 – 18 September 2025: International Congress on Biological Control

Costa Rica 16 – 18 Sep 2025

Agromultiservicios was present at the International Congress on Biological Control: Towards Regenerative and Sustainable Agriculture, held at the CATIE Campus, Turrialba. This event brought together international experts, producers, and innovative private companies implementing biological control strategies in Latin America and the Caribbean. The team's participation enabled technical exchange and engagement with companies and sector stakeholders interested in sustainable agricultural solutions, as well as the identification of potential clients and collaboration opportunities in research, development, and registration of bioproducts.

Chile 26 – 28 August 2025: ENEXPRO 2025 “Diversify Your Market”

Chile 26 – 28 Aug 2025

Agromultiservicios participated in ENEXPRO 2025 “Diversify Your Market,” the main export event organized by ProChile, held in Antofagasta, Santiago, and Concepción. During the event, the team actively participated in business meetings with Chilean companies interested in marketing their products in Costa Rica. This participation allowed the identification of potential clients and the exploration of business opportunities related to research projects and registration processes, strengthening the company's position as a technical and regulatory partner for entering the Costa Rican market.

Costa Rica 10 – 11 July 2025: Agrotransformación 2025

Costa Rica 10 – 11 Jul 2025

Agromultiservicios participated in Agrotransformación 2025, an event promoted by the DESCUBRE program and led by the Costa Rican Foreign Trade Promoter (PROCOMER), held at the TEC campus in San Carlos. During the event, the company had a stand where it attended to people interested in its services and interacted with agricultural sector stakeholders, entrepreneurs, input developers, and companies linked to the application of agricultural technologies such as drones. Additionally, the team participated in a bootcamp focused on generating solutions to real problems in the agricultural sector, which strengthened knowledge exchange and engagement with potential clients and strategic partners.

Honduras 8 – 9 May 2025: AGROMERCADOS Honduras 2025

Honduras 8 – 9 May 2025

Agromultiservicios participated in the 6th edition of AGROMERCADOS Honduras 2025, a national and international fair held at the Expocentro in San Pedro Sula, bringing together different actors in the agricultural and livestock value chain in Central America. During the event, PROCOMER Costa Rica coordinated a business meeting agenda that allowed the team to meet with companies potentially interested in marketing agricultural inputs in Costa Rica and developing research projects. This participation facilitated the identification of potential clients and real opportunities for commercial and technical collaboration in the agricultural sector.
