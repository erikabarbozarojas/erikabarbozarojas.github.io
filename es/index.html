About Agromultiservicios

Discover our History

Costa Rican company formed by professionals with more than ten years of experience in agricultural research nationally and internationally.

Agromultiservicios provides support and research services to renowned manufacturers of agricultural inputs. We made efficacy trials of various inputs such as fertilizers, invertebrates, biostimulants, fungicides, pesticides, microbiological, botanical extracts, and others; for internal research as well as for registration purposes.

Agromultiservicios also provides assistance in regulatory matters, preparing and managing the registration of agricultural inputs and plant varieties.

The company has meteorological stations distributed throughout the country and the facility of a greenhouse in the northern part of the country for make trials.

In Agromultiservicios we provide solutions and compete for quality. We guarantee the satisfaction of our clients under transparency and confidentiality.
