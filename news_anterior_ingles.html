Committed to innovation and continuous learning

Costa Rica Oct 25 2024

It was a pleasure to welcome our friends from AGROAMBIENTE-Ecuador, with whom we conducted technical visits to various projects in citrus, pineapple, and coffee. We thank engineers Guido Zurita and Andrés Zurita for sharing their experiences and knowledge. We hope our capacity for agricultural research and innovation continues to grow.

Costa Rica Jul 11 2024

The advisory team of Agromultiservicios participated in the 2024 edition of AGROTRANSFORMACIÓN, an event organized by the Foreign Trade Promoter (PROCOMER). At booth number 21, visitors had the opportunity to learn about our services and expand their network of contacts in research and regulatory affairs for agriculture in Costa Rica. The University EARTH hosted this important event.

Guatemala Mar 13-15 2024

In the city of Antigua Guatemala, the most significant agri-food sector fair in Central America, AGRITRADE 2024, took place. This event provided us with an opportunity to offer our research and regulatory affairs services to new companies and strengthen commercial ties with existing clients. Additionally, the event facilitated working meetings to reaffirm the implementation of new projects aimed at importing Guatemalan-made bioinputs into our country.

Costa Rica Feb 20 2024

We were present at the VIII Edition of Enlace Comercial held at the Crown Plaza Hotel, Corobicí. The eighth edition of Enlace Comercial was organized by the Costa Rican Foreign Trade Chamber (Crecex), where we held meetings with the commercial offices of the embassies of Israel, Spain, India, the Netherlands, and the United Kingdom. This activity allowed us to introduce ourselves in other markets and strengthen relationships with countries such as Israel, with whom we already have an excellent commercial relationship.

Costa Rica Set 2023

As part of the 35th anniversary of CIPROC at the University of Costa Rica, our team participated with a stand where event attendees had the opportunity to learn about our services and clarify any questions regarding the research and registration process of agricultural inputs in our country. It was a highly productive event, as we were able to expand our network of contacts and client portfolio.

Honduras May 18-19 2023

Agromultiservicios participated in Agromercados, Honduras, an international fair organized by the Secretariat of Agriculture of Honduras, FUNDER (Foundation for Rural Business Development), and FENAGH (National Federation of Farmers and Ranchers of Honduras). This event serves as a pivotal gathering for diverse stakeholders in the agricultural sector—including producers, national and international buyers, agro-exporters, agro-industry, the financial sector, input suppliers, packaging and service providers, public entities, and cooperative ventures—to foster business opportunities. During the fair, we engaged with formulators and distributors of agricultural inputs, aiming to expand our market presence and stimulate research and registration of agricultural inputs that hold potential for the Costa Rican agriculture sector.
